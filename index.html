Products

  • ice cream

    Natural ice cream is our main product. We offer you to taste classic vanilla, chocolate and strawberry flavors. Also, to satisfy the tastes of each of our guests, this year we added ice cream with banana, coconut, pistachio, lime, pineapple, peach, raspberry and blueberry flavors. All ingredients are 100% natural

  • ice coffee

    we use 100% Arabica coffee from Guatemala, Honduras and Colombia. Honey processed, medium roasted. The quality of coffee is constantly monitored by our certified Q-graders. With the addition of our natural ice cream, we offer you to taste ice coffee and other cold drinks that you will never forget

  • milkshakes

    milkshake - a fragrant cold drink based on milk and ice cream with a delicate creamy taste and airy texture. For our youngest guests, we recommend tasting this drink with the addition of OREO cookies, marshmallows and milk chocolate, or less sweet vanilla + banana. Mmm... yammi)
